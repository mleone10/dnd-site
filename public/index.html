Features and Traits

  • Trance: (Elf) Rather than taking long rests, I go into a meditative trance for four hours per day.
  • Darkvision: (Elf) I can see in dim light within 60 feed as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light.
  • Fey Ancestry (Elf) I have advantage on saves against being charmed, and I cannot be put to sleep using magic.
  • Incisive Sense (Pallid Elf) I have advantage on Insight and Investigation ability checks.
  • Fey Presence (Archfey Patron) As an action, cause all creatures in a 10' cube in front of me to make a Wisdom save against my warlock spell save DC. On failure, they are charmed or frightened until the end of my next turn.
  • Misty Escape (Archfey Patron) When I take damage, use my reaction to turn invisible and teleport up to 60' to an unoccupied space I can see. Remain invisible until the start of my next turn or until I attack/cast a spell. Once per short/long rest.
  • Beguiling Defenses (Archfey Patron) I am immune to being charmed. When another creature tries to charm me, I can use my reaction to make them make a wisdom saving throw against my spell save DC. If they fail, they are charmed by me for one minute or until they take damage.
  • Researcher: (Sage) When I attempt to learn or recall a piece of lore, if I don't know it, I often know where and from whom I can obtain it. Usually this is a library, university, or other learned individual. The DM might rule that it is inaccessible or does not exist.

Invocations

  • Agonizing Blast: I add my charisma modifier to Eldritch Blast damage.
  • Grasp of Hadar: Once per turn when I hit a creature with Eldritch Blast, I can move that creature 10 feet straight toward me.
  • Repelling Blast: When I hit a creature with Eldritch Blast, I can push them 10 feet away from me.
  • Mask of Many Faces: I can cast disguise self at will without expending a spell slot.
  • Book of Ancient Secrets: I can inscribe rituals in my Book of Shadows. Rituals can be from any class's spell list, can only be cast as rituals, and must be of a level equal to or less than half of my warlock level (rounded up).
